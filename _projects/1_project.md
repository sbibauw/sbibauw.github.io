Doctoral research project at KU Leuven and UCLouvain. Funded by a [SENESCYT](https://www.educacionsuperior.gob.ec/) scholarship (Ecuador).

Focused on the design and evaluation of task-based dialogue systems (chatbots, conversational agents...) for language learning.

**Researcher**: Serge Bibauw.

**Supervisors**: [Piet Desmet](https://itec.kuleuven-kulak.be/?page_id=2416) (itec, KU Leuven) and [Thomas François](https://cental.uclouvain.be/team/tfrancois/) (CENTAL/TeAMM, UCLouvain)
